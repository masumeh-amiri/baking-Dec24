Delicious Cake Yazdi

Persian Cardamom-Roswasser Muffins.
A recipe that's easy and perfect for every occasion.

About the Recipe

This cupcake comes from Yazd, a city in central Iran that is over 5,000 years old. Yazd is famous for its delicious sweets, silk fabrics, and unique buildings like Zoroastrian fire temples, mosques, and wind towers. These wind towers are clever structures that help keep buildings cool by catching breezes and releasing hot air. For Iranians, this sweet treat brings back warm memories and often leads to nostalgic stories of childhood days in Iran.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cups of sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of rose water
  • 1 packet of baking powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of natural yogurt
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 heaped teaspoon of cardamom powder
  • e.g. sesame seeds or chopped almonds,rosins ...
  • 12 paper cups

Cake Yazdi Recipe

Working time: approx. 30 minutes

Cooking/baking time: approx. 25 minutes

Total time: approx. 60 minutes

Preparation Steps

Steps

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

Preheat the oven to 180Â°C (top/bottom heat)

Use a 200 ml cup to measure the ingredients.

Step 2

In a bowl, beat the eggs with a mixer.

Step 3

Add the sugar to the eggs and mix well.

Step 4

Mix in the oil, rose water, and cardamom powder.

Step 5

In a separate bowl, combine the baking powder and flour.

Step 6

Sift the flour mixture into the egg mixture and stir until smooth.



Serving Suggestion

Enjoy your Cake Yazdi with a cup of Persian black tea!



Baking Steps

  1. Line a 12-cup muffin tray with paper cases.
  2. Divide the dough evenly between the cases, filling each up to 3/4 full.
  3. Sprinkle sesame seeds or your choice of nuts/seeds on top.
  4. Place the tray in the oven and bake for 20-30 minutes. Use a skewer to test for doneness.
  5. Once baked, let the muffins cool on a wire rack.
